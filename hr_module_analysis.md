## Summary of Critical Improvements Needed

The application has a solid foundation with good UI/UX and modular architecture, but several critical gaps prevent it from being a complete HR solution:

1. **Employee Self-Service**: Currently limited functionality for employees to manage their own information
2. **Manager Self-Service**: Limited tools for managers to oversee their teams
3. **Reporting and Analytics**: Missing comprehensive reporting capabilities
4. **Cross-Module Integration**: Limited connection between different HR functions
5. **Mobile Responsiveness**: Need for mobile-optimized interfaces
6. **Workflow Automation**: Limited automated processes between modules
7. **Advanced Security**: Need for enhanced security features

These improvements would significantly enhance the usability and completeness of the HR system for SME operations.
