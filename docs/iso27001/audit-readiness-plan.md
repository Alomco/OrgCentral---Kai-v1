# ISO 27001 Audit Readiness Plan

## Stage 1 readiness checklist
- ISMS scope statement approved.
- Information security policy published.
- Risk assessment completed and current.
- SoA updated and approved.
- Internal audit performed.
- Management review completed.

## Stage 2 readiness checklist
- Controls implemented and operational.
- Evidence of effectiveness available.
- Staff training records current.
- Incidents logged with post-incident reviews.
- Metrics collected for at least 3 months.

## Audit pack
- Scope, policies, risk register, SoA, internal audit report, management review minutes.
