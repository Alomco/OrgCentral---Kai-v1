# ISO 27001 Risk Assessment Workflow

## Workflow
1. Define scope and assets.
2. Identify threats and vulnerabilities.
3. Score risk (likelihood x impact).
4. Select treatment (mitigate, transfer, accept, avoid).
5. Record in risk register with owner and due date.
6. Track residual risk and review effectiveness.

## Cadence
- Quarterly update or on significant change.
- Immediate update after major incident or new critical system.

## Tooling
- python .github/skills/information-security-manager-iso27001/scripts/risk_assessment.py --scope "orgcentral"

## Evidence
- Risk register exports
- Treatment plans and approvals
- Residual risk review notes
