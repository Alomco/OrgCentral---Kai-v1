# ISO 27001 Governance Plan

## Management review cadence
- Annual formal management review meeting.
- Quarterly interim updates for KPI and KRI tracking.

## KPI and KRI
- Compliance score by domain
- Patch SLA adherence
- Incident volume and severity trend
- Access review completion rate
- Audit finding closure rate

## Internal audit schedule
- Semi-annual internal audit covering all Annex A domains.
- Spot checks quarterly for high-risk controls.

## Evidence
- Management review minutes
- KPI and KRI dashboards
- Internal audit reports and remediation tracking
