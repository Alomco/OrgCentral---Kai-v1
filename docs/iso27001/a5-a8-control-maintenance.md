# ISO 27001 A.5 to A.8 Control Maintenance

## Coverage goals
- Maintain implementation status and evidence for A.5 to A.8 controls.
- Verify effectiveness with defined metrics.

## Control areas, verification, and metrics
| Area | Example controls | Verification steps | Metrics |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| A.5 Organizational | Policies, roles, asset ownership | Annual policy review, org chart validation | Policy review completion rate |
| A.6 People | Screening, training, termination | Training completion, HR offboarding checks | Training completion >= 95 percent |
| A.7 Physical | Access control, secure areas | Badge access review, visitor logs | Physical access review quarterly |
| A.8 Technological | Malware, vulnerabilities, crypto | Scan results, endpoint coverage, TLS scans | Patch SLA compliance, crypto coverage |

## Evidence cadence
- Monthly: A.8 metrics and technical reports.
- Quarterly: A.5 to A.7 reviews.
- Annual: full policy review and management sign-off.
