# ISO 27001 Operational Plan

## Incident response
- Detection and reporting within 15 minutes of discovery.
- Triage within 1 hour for high severity incidents.
- Post-incident review completed within 5 business days.

## Vulnerability management
- Weekly authenticated scans for critical systems.
- Remediate critical and high issues within 30 days.
- Maintain exception register with compensating controls.

## Access reviews
- Monthly review of privileged access.
- Quarterly review of application access groups.
- Record approvals and removals in ticketing system.

## Training
- Annual security awareness training for all staff.
- Role-based training for privileged roles within 30 days of role change.

## Evidence
- Incident tickets, scan reports, access review logs, training records.
